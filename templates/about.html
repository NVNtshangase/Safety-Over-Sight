About SOS: Safety Over Sight

SOS: Safety Over Sight is a smart security and attendance tracking app designed to ensure the safety of students as they enter and exit school premises. Our system leverages advanced biometric fingerprint technology to provide real-time updates on student movements, offering peace of mind for parents and school administrators.

Our Mission

To create a safe, secure, and transparent environment for students, ensuring that parents and school authorities are informed and updated every step of the way.

Key Features:

  • Fingerprint Registration: Accurate biometric tracking when students board buses, enter school gates, and leave the premises.
  • Real-Time Notifications: Instant alerts for parents and school staff whenever a student arrives or departs.
  • Enhanced Safety: Continuous communication and tracking to minimize the risks of student abductions or irregular activities.
  • User-Friendly: Easy-to-use Android interface designed for parents, teachers, and administrators to monitor student attendance seamlessly.
  • Data Security: Encrypted data storage and secure login with multi-factor authentication to protect student information.

Why SOS?

At SOS, we understand the increasing concerns about student safety in today's world. Our app is designed to provide a layer of security, ensuring students are always safe, and parents stay informed.
